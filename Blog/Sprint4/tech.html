Problem solving!

Well most of my problems had to do with writing at first but as javascript was introduced i started to have problems with the code side of things. The biggest problem for me was the magic symbols.

I mean these things ++ === >< ! &&. They all had different combinations and were used in completely different ways. When I say that they confused me I don't mean like I saw them and I couldn't read them as part of the code. I knew what they meant and what they did endavidgly but put them together and being used in a loop.

It took me way too long to figure out how to use them, I still don't have the greatest grasp of how to use them but after using them first hand and researching them I have a much better understanding of the symbols.

It felt so odd to not understand something so basic, and I learnt that certain things mean different things under different context.i.e this => is an arrow >= this means greater than or equal to.
