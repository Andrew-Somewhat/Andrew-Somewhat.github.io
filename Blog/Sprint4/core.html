Neuroplasticity is the measurement of the brain's capacity to improve.

Spoilers its seemingly endless.The benefits of neuroplasticity are that it shows that your brain can improve even after its fully developed.

And I think it's pretty obvious as to how that would affect your studies, but in case it isn't it (since I didn't give a full explanation) if you keep practicing something you'll get better at it.I’m sure that you've heard similar sayings.

Growth Mindset!

Growth mindset is the belief that you still have room to grow and reach new heights in terms of ye mind.

And simply believing you can grow, or at least being open to the idea that you can improve and overcome a difficult task greatly helps learning capacity and rate.
